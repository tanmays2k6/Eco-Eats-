Frequently Asked Questions

How do you measure the social impact of your platform? +

We measure social impact by tracking the amount of food rescued, the number of meals distributed, and the reduction in greenhouse gas emissions from diverted food waste. Additionally, we gather feedback from NGOs and communities that benefit from the donations.

How do you help reduce food waste? +

We connect grocery stores, restaurants, and other food businesses with NGOs and charities to redistribute surplus food. This prevents food from ending up in landfills and instead provides meals to those in need.

Can I list my business as a food donor? +

Yes, businesses can sign up on our platform to become food donors. Once registered, they can list surplus food items available for donation, and we will handle the redistribution process.

How does your food redistribution process work? +

Our platform allows food donors to list surplus food items. NGOs can view and request these items, and our delivery network ensures the food reaches the intended recipients in a timely manner.

How do you prioritize which food to donate first? +

We prioritize food donations based on expiry dates, ensuring that items with the shortest shelf life are distributed first to minimize waste and maximize usability.

How does the delivery system work for food donations? +

Our delivery system involves a network of logistics partners who coordinate pickups from food donors and drop-offs to NGOs. We use real-time tracking to ensure timely and efficient deliveries.
